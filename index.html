Welcome to the official website of the Hindu Students' Association at the University of Rochester.

ABOUT US

Founded in 2002, HSA is an undergraduate-led group on campus open to students of all religions. While we provide an avenue for Hindus to nurture their customs, traditions and religious practices through temple visits, discussions, festival celebrations, etc, HSA also is an educational resource for anyone in their quest of Hinduism, eager to experience the essence of a community built surrounding a religious commonality!
